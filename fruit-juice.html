Fruit Juice

INGREDIENTS

500ml carrot juice, chilled
200g pineapple (fresh or canned)
2 bananas, broken into chunks
small piece ginger, peeled
20g cashew nuts
juice 1 lime

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Put the ingredients in a blender and whizz until smooth. Drink straight away or pour into a bottle to drink on the go. Will keep in the fridge for a day.
